Annie Stephenson

My interest in writing started with short stories. For the past few years, most of my writing has been about science, whether through papers, communication pieces, or talks. I try to see all of these as forms of storytelling, an approach I picked up from mentors and from books on science writing.

In 2017, I co-founded Softbites, a science communication blog that explains soft matter physics papers at an early undergraduate level. During my years as managing editor, I guided pieces through our editorial process, edited many myself, and occasionally contributed my own. I later led several semester-long science communication workshops at Harvard, and was invited to write an article for Physics Today, linked below.
