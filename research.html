Annie Stephenson

Core questions that motivate my work:

  • How can we build and validate models that link individual behavior to emergent patterns in social systems of humans and/or AI agents?
  • How do design features like frictions or incentives shape collective intelligence, cooperation, and coordination in social systems?
  • What new dynamics and cultural shifts will emerge as AI becomes embedded in human society?


My background is in physics, where I studied light-matter interactions in disordered systems. I relied on established results of Maxwell's equations and effective medium theory as foundations to build statistical models. In human systems, equivalent ground truths are not so well-established. But, surprisingly, consistent patterns still emerge, like organizational scaling laws and bursty communication. Now, I use machine learning and mechanistic modeling to study collective behavior in social systems of humans and AI, from Reddit communities to multi-agent AI simulations.
